My Favorite Anime

I first started watching anime during Covid-19, and ever since that time, I've watched more than 30 anime series. Here are my top 3 favorites.

On the thrid spot we have Naruto since its one of my favorite anime that I've watched. It's in my third spot because I enjoy watching every fight and the story it tells. It also led me to discover similar anime.

On the second spot Bleach is an anime with a very interesting story and epic sword fights. Its fights and animation are why it holds the second spot on my list.

Yu-Gi-Oh is my number one favorite because I started watching and playing it in 4th grade. I still collect and treasure my cards, with Yu-Gi-Oh 5Ds being my favorite series.
